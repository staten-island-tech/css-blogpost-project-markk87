Favorite Travel Destinations

The following places are 3 of the most famous current tourist destinations

#3

Cancun, Mexico is best known for its strangely shaped structure. There is a massive pool in between the narrow parts of the "island", which makes the experience even more pleasant.

#2

London, United Kingdom has many attractions for city-lovers. Some of the most popular ones include: The Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, London Eye, and more!

#1

The most popular and loved city to visit is Dubai, UAE. Standing there is the tallest building in the world, the Burj Khalifa. The entertainment there is unlimited, and best of all: there's no property tax! Yay!
